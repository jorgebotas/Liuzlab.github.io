title: "Lab Awarded $1M NSF Grant"
date: 2025-03-15
summary: "The Liu Lab has received a grant from the National Science Foundation to study robust computer vision systems."

We are thrilled to announce that the Liu Lab has been awarded a $1M grant from the NSF. This funding will support our research on making computer vision systems more robust to adversarial attacks and distribution shifts over the next three years.
