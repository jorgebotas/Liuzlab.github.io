NIH OT2 grant developing a robust framework for validation and replication of autism data science models through multi-modal, cross-institutional approach. Total award: $4,853,391 (FY2025). Leverages comprehensive datasets from Texas Children's Hospital, SPARK genetic cohort, and other repositories using advanced AI/ML methods.
