NIH R01 grant developing advanced and automated computational platforms for NMR metabolomics biomarker discovery. Total award: $356,625 (FY2017). This project develops novel algorithms (NMRQuant and SPA-STOCSY) for automated identification and quantification of metabolites, substantially improving accuracy and reproducibility of NMR analysis.
