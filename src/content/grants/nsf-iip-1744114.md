NSF I-Corps grant for customer discovery and commercialization of augmented reality-based therapy delivery system for treating specific phobias. Total award: $50,000 (FY2017). This project validated product-market fit for AR platform enabling clinicians to deliver exposure therapy in a more acceptable and cost-effective manner.
