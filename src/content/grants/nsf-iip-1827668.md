NSF Partnerships for Innovation - Technology Translation grant for developing an augmented-reality platform to treat phobias and anxiety in children with Autism Spectrum Disorder. Total award: $199,791 (FY2018). This innovative project combines computational approaches with therapeutic applications using adaptive learning and computer vision technologies.
