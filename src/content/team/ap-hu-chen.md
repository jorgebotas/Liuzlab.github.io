Hu is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Pediatrics at Baylor College of Medicine. He received his training in computational biology and cancer genomics. At Baylor and NRI, he focuses on developing algorithms for rare disease diagnosis, integrative multi-omics characterization of neurological disorders, and the application of natural language processing (NLP) techniques in medical informatics.
